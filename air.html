Air Pollution

“Love is in the air but the air is highly polluted” ― Amit Abraham

What is Air Pollution?

Air Pollution is a type of pollution caused by releasing hurtful gas pollutants, such as carbon dioxide and carbon monoxide, to the atmosphere.

What Are the Sources Of It?

Air pollution can be caused by the burning of fossil fuels from cars and factories.

Why Should You Care?

Air Pollution is able to cause many hurtful diseases (heart diseases, stroke, lung cancer). It can also cause an increase in blood pressure to children and decrease in birth rate. Air pollution can also cause acid rain, which can disrupt ecosystems and agriculture.
