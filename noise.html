Noise Pollution

“No man should live where he can hear his neighbor's dog bark.” ― Nathaniel Macon

What is A Noise Pollution?

Noise pollution is "harmful or annoying levels of noise, as from airplanes, industry, etc."

What Should You Do?

It is in the power of the people to impose a ban on the action of certain facilities that produce noise pollution. An example includes the testing and commercial use of supersonic jets during the 1980s; the sonic boom produced by these aircraft would cause damage in the eardrums and of windows, ultimatley disrupting the peace of the community.
